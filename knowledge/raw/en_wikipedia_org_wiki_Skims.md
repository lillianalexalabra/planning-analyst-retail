Skims

**Skims** is an American [shapewear](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shapewear "Shapewear") and [clothing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clothing "Clothing") brand co-founded by [Kim Kardashian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kim_Kardashian "Kim Kardashian"), [Emma Grede](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emma_Grede "Emma Grede"), and Jens Grede.[\[1\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-cnbc-11-21-1) Skims emphasizes [body positivity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Body_positivity "Body positivity") and inclusivity, offering a full range of [inclusive sizing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inclusive_sizing "Inclusive sizing").[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-voguebusiness-02-22-2) The company is valued at over $5 billion as of 2025.[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-nytimes-04-21-3)[\[4\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-4)

## History

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Skims&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: History")\]

The company was сo-founded in June 2019 by American media personality and businesswoman [Kim Kardashian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kim_Kardashian "Kim Kardashian") and Swedish entrepreneur Jens Grede.[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-bloomberg-01-22-5) Kardashian's then-husband [Kanye West](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kanye_West "Kanye West") was credited as being her "Ghost [Creative Director](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Creative_director "Creative director")" and for creating the company's logo.[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-6)[\[7\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-7) Prior to creation of this line, Kardashian was inspired by her own creative work of altering clothes, stating she "\[has been\] designing shapewear for 15 years".[\[8\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-8)

Skims' initial launch resulted in more than $2 million in profit and the company selling out of merchandise in 10 minutes.[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-:0-9) The company claims to have sold more than three million products in its first year in business.[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-voguebusiness-02-22-2)

In April 2021, the company was reported to be worth over $1.6 billion.[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-nytimes-04-21-3) After further investments, the valuation of Skims was reported at $3.2 billion in January 2022.[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-bloomberg-01-22-5)[\[10\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-:1-10)

In March 2022, Skims made it on [_TIME_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_(magazine) "Time (magazine)")'s List of 100 Most Influential Companies of the year 2022.[\[11\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-11)

In October 2023, Skims announced the company's first venture into menswear with a collection of underwear, t-shirts, socks, and tank tops designed for men. [\[12\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-12)[\[13\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-13)

In December 2024, actress [Nia Long](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nia_Long "Nia Long") was featured in a Skims shapewear campaign.[\[14\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-14)

## Products

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Skims&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Products")\]

Skims produces clothes in sizes ranging from XXS to 5XL.[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-voguebusiness-02-22-2) The brand's shapewear was designed to accommodate different skin tones and comes in nine shades.[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-voguebusiness-02-22-2) In addition to shapewear, Skims has released an array of collections in loungewear, [lingerie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lingerie "Lingerie"), and accessories, including bras, underwear, bodysuits, dresses, shorts, sweatpants, tops, hoodies, sweaters, and slides.[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-voguebusiness-02-22-2)[\[15\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-15)

The brand originally only sold to consumers via their website, skims.com, but availability later extended to a variety of online websites and in-store at [Nordstrom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nordstrom "Nordstrom").[\[16\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-16)[\[17\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-17)

### Collaborations

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Skims&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Collaborations")\]

In 2021, the company established a partnership with the [United States Olympics team](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_at_the_Olympics "United States at the Olympics"), providing Team USA with Skims-branded [athleisure](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Athleisure "Athleisure") for its athletes. The athletes wore the clothing during the [2020 Summer Olympics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2020_Summer_Olympics "2020 Summer Olympics") and [Paralympics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2020_Summer_Paralympics "2020 Summer Paralympics") in Tokyo, and at the [2022 Winter Olympics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2022_Winter_Olympics "2022 Winter Olympics") in Beijing.[\[18\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-18)[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-bloomberg-01-22-5)

In October 2021, the company collaborated with [Italian fashion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italian_fashion "Italian fashion") house [Fendi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fendi "Fendi"), creating a [capsule collection](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capsule_wardrobe "Capsule wardrobe") of FENDI x SKIMS branded shapewear, leather dresses, and swimsuits.[\[19\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-19)[\[20\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-wwd-10-21-20) The collaboration was inspired by a Fendi collection previously released in 1978 by [Karl Lagerfeld](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karl_Lagerfeld "Karl Lagerfeld").[\[20\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-wwd-10-21-20)[\[21\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-21) [Bloomberg News](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bloomberg_News "Bloomberg News") reported that the collaboration generated an estimated US$3 million in sales within 10 minutes of its release.[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-bloomberg-01-22-5)

In September 2023, the company and the [National Basketball Association (NBA)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Basketball_Association "National Basketball Association") announced a multiyear partnership in making Skims the official underwear partner of the NBA, the [Women's National Basketball League (WNBA)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Women%27s_National_Basketball_Association "Women's National Basketball Association"), and [USA Basketball](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USA_Basketball "USA Basketball").[\[22\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-22) As part of the partnership, Skims received media exposure across several league platforms, including on-court virtual signage during NBA and WNBA broadcasts, as well as on the leagues' official [social and digital platforms](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_media "Social media"). The company and its products were also highlighted at USA Basketball marquee events, including the USA Basketball Showcase, which features USA men's and women's national team exhibition games before major international competitions.[\[23\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-23)[\[24\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-24)

In January 2026, announced Nike x Skims Spring 2026 collection, highlighting its ballet‑inspired designs, new Rift Satin footwear, and a campaign featuring K-pop artist [LISA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lisa_(rapper) "Lisa (rapper)").[\[25\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-CasawiNikeSkims2026-25)

## Controversies

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Skims&action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Controversies")\]

The company was originally named **Kimono Intimates** by Kardashian and Jens Grede.[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-bloomberg-01-22-5)[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-nytimes-04-21-3) The company's initial name was criticized by critics which argued that it disrespected Japanese culture and ignored the significance behind the country's [traditional outfit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kimono "Kimono"). The mayor of [Kyoto](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kyoto "Kyoto"), [Daisaku Kadokawa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daisaku_Kadokawa "Daisaku Kadokawa"), wrote to Kardashian asking her to reconsider the company name and trademark for Kimono. Later, Kardashian announced that she would change the company's name to **Skims**.[\[26\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-26)[\[27\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-27)

## Financials

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Skims&action=edit&section=6 "Edit section: Financials")\]

Skims' initial launch resulted in more than $2 million in profit and the company selling out of merchandise in 10 minutes.[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-:0-9) The company claims to have sold more than 3 million products in its first year in business.[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-voguebusiness-02-22-2)

In April 2021, the company was reported to be worth over $1.6 billion.[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-nytimes-04-21-3) In January 2022, Skims was reported over to be worth $3.2 billion. After further investments, the valuation increased to $4 billion in July 2023.[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-bloomberg-01-22-5)[\[10\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-:1-10)

As of 2024, Skims generated around $1 billion in net sales.[\[30\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-30)

## Awards

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Skims&action=edit&section=7 "Edit section: Awards")\]

In 2021, founder Kim Kardashian was given a "Brand Innovator" award for her work on Skims by [_Wall Street Journal Magazine_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WSJ "WSJ")'s Innovator Awards.[\[31\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-31)

In November 2022, Skims received the inaugural "Innovation Award" presented by [Amazon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amazon_(company) "Amazon (company)") Fashion at the 2022 [CFDA Fashion Awards](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Council_of_Fashion_Designers_of_America "Council of Fashion Designers of America"). Cofounders Kim Kardashian, Emma Grede, and Jens Grede accepted the award at [Casa Cipriani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Casa_Cipriani "Casa Cipriani") in [Manhattan, New York](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manhattan "Manhattan").[\[32\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skims#cite_note-32)
